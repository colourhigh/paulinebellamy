Bellamy's confident, prolific practice has seen her work shown widely and held in numerous collections. She has gained a reputation as an expressive painter of South Island landscape, figures, portraits and etchings. Bellamy celebrates the unassuming, lesser-known side of the New Zealand character with a generous, observational perspective that is distinctly hers.
